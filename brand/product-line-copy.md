## Voice Rules Applied

**Corporate guide (Anna/CMO):** Transparent challenger positioning. Proof-first. Sales-enablement focused. Call out industry problems directly. Every claim backed by specific evidence.

**Personal voice (Tina):** Direct, confident, warm. No throat-clearing. No defensive framing. Concise beats verbose.

**Universal rules:**
- No em dashes (commas, periods, colons, or parentheses instead)
- No "solutions," "comprehensive," "scalable," "innovative" without proof
- Replace "quality leads" with "verified, sales-ready leads"
- Replace "advanced targeting" with "behavior-based precision"
- Never name competitors directly in marketing content (use category references)
- Structure: problem callout, then proof point, then specific outcome
