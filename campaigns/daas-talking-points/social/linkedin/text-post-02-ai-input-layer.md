Every AI agent your company deploys needs one thing to be useful: proprietary data.

Not a better model. Not a fancier interface. Data.

Ali Ghodsi, CEO of Databricks: "The next wave of AI success is going to be completely dependent on structured data."

Bain & Company: "Your data is your moat. While models such as GPT-4o are everywhere, the real value lies in the proprietary data you own."

Teddie Wardi at Insight Partners: "There's a real data-layer bottleneck in enterprise AI deployments that needs addressing first."

AI commoditizes the software layer. It increases demand for the data layer.

More AI systems deployed = more demand for proprietary data inputs. That's not speculation. It's already showing up in the multiples.

The question for every B2B platform right now: Do you own your data layer, or are you renting the same data as your competitors?
