SSP Evaluation — B2B Audience PMP

Index Exchange vs. PubMatic

Prepared by pharosIQ Data Products & Partnerships  |  April 2026
Recommendation

Lead with Index Exchange for the initial TTD PMP deal. Their OpenPath partnership with The Trade Desk gives your deal IDs a structural routing advantage. Establish a PubMatic relationship in parallel as a secondary deal ID for scale. Neither requires LiveRamp.

Where Each Platform Wins

Index Exchange

Stronger For This Deal

  • OpenPath partner with The Trade Desk — direct pipe, fewer hops, lower supply chain fees
  • Curated Marketplaces program built for non-publisher data owners
  • Industry-leading fee transparency; clean supply chain story post Publicis/TTD audit controversy
  • Native UID2 support — no additional identity infrastructure needed
  • Premium publisher quality with strong display and video inventory
PubMatic

Stronger For Scale

  • Significantly larger CTV/OTT publisher footprint
  • Greater raw impression volume — better for scaling past initial deal
  • Audience Encore data collaboration product for publisher-side monetization
  • PubMatic Connect identity graph supplements UID2 to improve match rates
  • Publicly traded (PUBM) — accessible investor-grade transparency

How the Deal Works (No LiveRamp Required)

  1. 1
    Define the audience segment Companies and contacts matching your intent surge signal, matched to target titles. Deliver as SHA-256 hashed emails.
  2. 2
    Encode to Unified ID 2.0 (UID2) Hash the contact emails to UID2 tokens via the open-source UID2 operator. Free. No LiveRamp needed. Index Exchange and TTD both accept UID2 natively.
  3. 3
    IX creates the Deal ID IX packages your UID2-matched segment against their publisher inventory. They generate a Deal ID string (e.g., ix-pharosiq-b2b-intent-q2-2026) with a $5-10 CPM floor, weekly refresh, and your specified flight dates.
  4. 4
    Client activates in The Trade Desk Client enters the Deal ID in their TTD seat under Inventory > My Deals. Assigns to their campaign. Sets bid parameters. No special integration on their end.
  5. 5
    Measure via TTD native tools TTD Universal Pixel for site conversions, or offline conversion upload using hashed emails. No third-party attribution vendor required.
What to bring to the Index Exchange conversation: pharosIQ data asset (360M+ B2B contacts, 650+ intent categories, first-party signal), one client with $300K media budget on TTD, hashed email delivery ready, target CPM $5-10, weekly refresh cadence. Ask for: Curated Marketplace deal, open web + video, client's TTD seat ID to route the Deal ID.

Fastest path to a warm intro: If your client's agency or trading desk holds an IX seat, have them make the introduction. IX accelerates onboarding for existing buyer relationships.
