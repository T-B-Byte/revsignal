PMP Infrastructure Evaluation — B2B Audience Activation

Index Exchange vs. LiveRamp vs. Permutive

Prepared by pharosIQ Data Products & Partnerships  |  April 2026
Recommendation

Lead with Index Exchange for the initial TTD PMP deal. Their OpenPath partnership with The Trade Desk gives deal IDs a structural routing advantage, and their Curated Marketplaces program is purpose-built for non-publisher data owners. UID2-hashed emails natively — no LiveRamp required. Revisit LiveRamp at scale (5+ clients). Permutive is a publisher tool and does not map to pharosIQ's data-owner use case.

LiveRamp cost reality check: $100K onboarding + $15K/month minimum = $280K in year one before a single impression runs. On a $300K client deal, that leaves $20K of margin to cover data costs, operations, and rev-share. LiveRamp becomes viable at 5+ clients where the fixed cost amortizes. Not deal one.
Permutive category mismatch: Permutive is a publisher-side DMP — it helps media companies and content publishers package and monetize their own first-party audiences. pharosIQ is a data owner and seller, not a publisher. Permutive is designed for the other side of this transaction. Notably, Permutive's own documentation describes ingesting second-party data via LiveRamp — they work alongside LiveRamp, not against it.

Where Each Platform Fits

Index Exchange is an SSP. LiveRamp is an identity and data onboarding platform. Permutive is a publisher DMP. Three different layers of the adtech stack — only IX maps directly to pharosIQ's data-owner distribution need.

Index Exchange

Recommended: First Deal

  • OpenPath TTD partner — direct pipe, fewer hops, lower supply chain fees
  • Curated Marketplaces built for non-publisher data owners
  • Best-in-class fee transparency post Publicis/TTD audit controversy
  • Native UID2 — no identity infrastructure needed
  • No upfront cost; ~10-15% take rate on media only
  • Taylor has prior relationship — fastest path to deal
LiveRamp

Revisit at Scale (5+ Clients)

  • Largest identity graph — best match rates at scale
  • RampID: deepest TTD integration, frequency capping, omnichannel addressability
  • Established data marketplace with direct DSP demand
  • $100K onboarding + $15K/month minimum
  • Additional rev-share on data marketplace distribution
  • Cost unworkable for first deal at $300K budget
Permutive

Wrong Layer of the Stack

  • Publisher-side DMP: helps media companies activate their own first-party audiences
  • Privacy-first, cookieless, on-device/edge computing architecture
  • Used by major publishers (The Guardian, SHE Media) to package and sell audiences
  • pharosIQ is a data owner, not a publisher — wrong role for this tool
  • Permutive integrates with LiveRamp (ingest via LR) — they're complementary, not competitive
  • Future angle: data clean room collaboration with Permutive publishers

How the Deal Works via Index Exchange (No LiveRamp Required)

  1. 1
    Define the audience segment Companies and contacts matching your intent surge signal, matched to target titles. Deliver as SHA-256 hashed emails.
  2. 2
    Encode to Unified ID 2.0 (UID2) Hash contact emails to UID2 tokens via the open-source UID2 operator. Free. No LiveRamp needed. Index Exchange and TTD both accept UID2 natively.
  3. 3
    IX creates the Deal ID IX packages your UID2-matched segment against their publisher inventory and generates a Deal ID (e.g., ix-pharosiq-b2b-intent-q2-2026) with a $5-10 CPM floor, weekly refresh, and your specified flight dates.
  4. 4
    Client activates in The Trade Desk Client enters the Deal ID under Inventory > My Deals in their TTD seat. Assigns to a campaign, sets bid parameters. No special integration required on their end.
  5. 5
    Measure via TTD native tools TTD Universal Pixel for site conversions, or offline conversion upload via hashed emails. No third-party attribution vendor required.
What to bring to the Index Exchange conversation: pharosIQ data asset (125.3M B2B contacts (last 90 days), 8,756 intent topics, first-party signal), one client with $300K media budget on TTD, hashed email delivery ready, target CPM $5-10, weekly refresh cadence. Ask for: Curated Marketplace deal, open web + video, client's TTD seat ID to route the Deal ID. Target rev-share: 10-15% with $3-5K/month minimum.

When LiveRamp makes sense: Once pharosIQ has 5+ active PMP clients, the $100K onboarding amortizes to $20K per client and RampID's match rate advantage becomes meaningful. Revisit after the first two deals close.

Permutive future angle: If pharosIQ wants to collaborate with premium publishers who use Permutive (The Guardian, major media brands), there's a clean room opportunity to layer pharosIQ B2B intent signals onto publisher first-party audiences. Not deal one infrastructure — a later expansion play.
