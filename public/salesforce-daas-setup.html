Context: pharosIQ is standing up a net-new DaaS (Data-as-a-Service) revenue line. This is a distinct sales motion from demand gen — longer cycles, data evaluations, legal review, annual contracts. It needs its own record type, stages, fields, and products in SFDC so it doesn't get mixed into existing pipeline reporting.
01

Opportunity Record Type

Action Required

Create a new Opportunity record type called "DaaS License" (or "Data License" — confirm naming convention with the team). This keeps DaaS deals out of demand gen and managed services reporting from day one.

Confirm: Are these stages added to the existing Stage picklist (scoped by record type) or a separate field? Prefer record-type-scoped picklist values if SFDC version supports it.

05

Billing & Contract Notes

Discuss
1
Payment Terms
Annual contracts, invoiced upfront (preferred) or quarterly. Net 30 standard. Multi-year discounts: 10% for 2-year, 15% for 3-year.
2
Revenue Recognition
Track both ACV (annual contract value) and TCV (total contract value) on the Opportunity. Finance will need ACV for quota credit and TCV for revenue rec on multi-year deals.
3
Renewal Tracking
90-day flag on Contract End Date. Confirm whether this should live on the Opportunity or on a linked Contract object. Ask if there is a renewal workflow already in use.
4
Billing Channel Split
Most DaaS products bill via pharosIQ invoicing (AR). AtlasIQ packs may route through Stripe. SFDC should track the billing channel so finance can reconcile — add a "Billing Channel" field (Picklist: pharosIQ Invoice, Stripe) if not already present.
5
OEM / Reseller Sub-Accounts
Some deals will have a reseller billing one entity while end-customers consume data through sub-accounts. Confirm the best way to model this: Account Hierarchy, or a custom lookup from the Opportunity to the end-customer account.
6
Quota & Reporting
DaaS pipeline should appear as a distinct line in pipeline reports and forecasts. Confirm the Opportunity Record Type filter will work for the CRO / CFO dashboard — or flag if a custom report type is needed.

Decisions Needed in This Meeting

Record type name confirmed: "DaaS License" vs. "Data License" vs. other
Stage picklist approach: record-type-scoped values vs. a separate field
Custom fields approved — any conflicts with existing Opportunity fields?
Products added to Standard Price Book or a new "DaaS" price book?
Renewal tracking: Opportunity-level or Contract object?
OEM / sub-account modeling: Account Hierarchy or custom lookup?
Timeline: when can this configuration be live in production?
