Program Tracks

Co-Branded

"Powered by pharosIQ" attribution

  • Wholesale pricing at 50% off standard Intelligence Suite rates
  • "Powered by pharosIQ" attribution on all reports
  • Licensing fee required (less than white-label; scaled to program value)
  • Annual minimum term
  • Invoiced through pharosIQ

White-Label / Private Label

Custom contract, negotiated per partner

  • Three-component contract structure:
  • 1. Private label fee (scaled to company size and program value)
  • 2. Monthly minimum against wholesale rates (50% off standard)
  • 3. Annual or multi-year term
  • No standard rate card. Every deal is different.
  • Invoiced through pharosIQ
A small agency and a global distribution platform are structured very differently. There is no default white-label price. All white-label deals require a direct conversation and a custom contract before any pricing is committed.
