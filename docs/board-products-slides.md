## Slide 2 — The Product Portfolio

**DaaS Licensing, Full Database** (4 additive tiers)

Enterprise data access, no target account list filter. Entry tier ($60K-$96K ACV) competes with Bombora. Enterprise tier ($1.2M+) embeds pharosIQ data inside partner platforms at scale.

**TAL-Matched Signal Delivery** (annual subscriptions)

Intent signals filtered to the buyer's target account list. Market, Buyer, and Pipeline Signal tiers. $48K-$180K ACV. Stripe-billed, annual term.

**AtlasIQ Intelligence Suite** (self-service reports)

On-demand account intelligence. Account Architect ($0 free tier to $5K packs), ABM Strategist ($1K per report), Deal DNA ($3.5K per report). Positions pharosIQ as a product company, not a services company.

**Jobson Title Expansion** (self-service)

Title variations engine. $500 per title, $100 per additional language. 15+ languages, all industries.

**Reseller Program** (partner channel)

Co-branded ("Powered by pharosIQ", 50% wholesale) and white-label tracks. Every white-label deal is a custom contract.
